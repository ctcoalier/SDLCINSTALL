What is CCUG?

We're a community of San Diego developers building with Claude and Claude Code — from weekend tinkerers to teams shipping in production. We meet to share what's working, swap prompts and workflows, and keep up with a fast-moving toolset. This site is where that knowledge lives between gatherings.
